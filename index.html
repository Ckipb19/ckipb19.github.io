About Me

Hello, My name is Christopher Besla.

Ever since He was a little boy he grew up with two forms of media, Books and Movies. Both of these things have become essential to who Chris was as a person and also as a digital author. Writing and filmmaking (especially video editing) are his greatest passions and crafts. He is currently more of a screenwriter when it comes to my writing craft as well as he has been working as a video editor for the past few years since 2020 on the Youtube Channel called CimplyKipster. Chris is currently have been working on a screenplay for since January 2022 that is called the Screwtape meetings which has devidied most of his attention and he hopes to finish it by March 2024 .

I have always valued the pursuit of excellence, not that you will be perfect or excellent all the time, but the pursuit of it and the need for it. This appears in his own . Another quality that I value is honesty and integrity in one's work or project. The reason is based in the idea that each person's work is apart of them and a person should stand up for their work especially if it faces opposition when people don't like very "nitpicky" things about it. These are some of the things i value in myself and try to hold myself to but also want the people I work with to value.
